Factory Plants

Maybricks factory built up area consists of 10 acres which are divided into 2 plants: Plant 1 and Plant 2. Both plants contribute to the total production of 300,000 pieces per day. With more than 30 years of experience in brick making industry, we are a group of brick makers with strong R&D capabilities. Our products are produced under the most stringent quality control to serve the needs and requirements of current market environment, governing bodies, legislation and regulations.
